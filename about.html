Biography

A career built step by step across multiple disciplines.

I started my professional career as a Graphic Designer from 2013 to 2015. That experience gave me a strong foundation in visual communication, presentation, and thinking about how people engage with digital work.

In 2020, I worked as a Junior Software Developer Intern at SoftifyBD. That role helped me grow my technical understanding and gave me a stronger view of how software is built behind the scenes.

Since 2022, I have been working at WPDeveloper on xCloud, helping customers with WordPress and server issues, migrations, bug verification, documentation, and product usability.

Today, I bring together support operations, product quality, clear communication, and a practical engineering mindset. I enjoy work that improves trust, removes friction, and makes technology easier to use.
